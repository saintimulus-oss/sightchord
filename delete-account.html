Delete Account

Use this page to understand how Chordest account deletion works and how to start a deletion request inside or outside the app.

Support Privacy Policy Open App

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Delete inside the app

  1. Open Chordest.
  2. Go to Main Menu > Settings > Account.
  3. Select Delete account.
  4. Enter your current password and confirm the deletion request.

What gets deleted

  • Your Chordest account in Firebase Authentication.
  • Your synced premium entitlement record stored for that account in Cloud Firestore.

What is not deleted automatically

  • Local practice settings, local progress, and other data stored only on your device. Remove those by uninstalling the app or clearing app data on that device.
  • Purchase history managed by Google Play or the App Store. Those records stay under the store provider's own policies and controls.

If you cannot access the app

If you can no longer sign in or no longer have access to a device with Chordest installed, use the support contact email shown on the store listing and request account deletion for the same email address used in the app.

Do not submit account deletion requests through the public GitHub issue tracker, because it is not intended for private account data handling.
