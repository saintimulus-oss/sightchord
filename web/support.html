Account deletion

If you can still access the app, go to Main Menu > Settings > Account and use the in-app Delete account action.

If you cannot access the app anymore, use the store listing support contact email for a private deletion request instead of the public issue tracker.

Open deletion instructions

What to include in a report

  • Your device model and OS version.
  • The app version and build number.
  • What you expected to happen and what happened instead.
  • A screenshot or screen recording if the issue is visual.
  • Whether the issue happens every time or only on certain progressions, lessons, or audio settings.

Known limits in the current release

  • Practice settings and progress are still local to the device.
  • Premium can sync across devices only when you sign in with the same Chordest account.
  • Some recent explanatory strings may still fall back to English in selected non-English locales.
  • Audio playback is optimized for the bundled local piano asset set and may sound different across speaker hardware.
