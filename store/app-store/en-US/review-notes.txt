Chordest is a local-first music practice app for chord study, improvisation, and harmonic vocabulary work.

Reviewer guidance:
- No sign-in or account setup is required.
- Core flows to test: Main Menu, Practice Generator, Smart Generator, Chord Analyzer, and Settings.
- The app stores settings and progress locally on the device.
- The current repository build does not use ads, account login, camera, microphone, or location access.

If a test device is muted, please enable audio output to verify piano playback and metronome behavior.
