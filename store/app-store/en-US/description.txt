Chordest helps you practice chords, harmony, and improvisation with a focused local-first workflow.

Generate progressions across all 12 roots, switch between free and key-aware modes, and explore weighted harmonic motion with Smart Generator. Use the built-in Chord Analyzer to inspect key center, Roman numerals, harmonic function, and summary output while you practice.

Features include:
- Chord practice across all 12 roots
- Key mode with diatonic Roman numerals
- Smart Generator for weighted harmonic motion
- Chord Analyzer with theory-oriented summaries
- Metronome, autoplay, BPM control, and notation options
- Localized UI for English, Spanish, Simplified Chinese, Japanese, and Korean

Chordest currently stores settings and progress on-device and does not require account creation.
