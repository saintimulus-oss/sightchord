Chordest is a local-first harmony practice app for improvisation, reading, and harmonic vocabulary work.

Practice across all 12 roots, switch between free and key-aware generation, and explore weighted harmonic motion with Smart Generator. Inspect progressions with the built-in Chord Analyzer, study harmonic function, and drill chord movement with metronome and autoplay tools.

Chordest includes:
- Free and key-based practice modes
- Smart harmonic progression generation
- Chord Analyzer summaries with harmonic context
- Tensions, inversions, slash chords, and alternate symbol styles
- Localized UI for English, Spanish, Simplified Chinese, Japanese, and Korean

The current release is local-first. Settings and progress stay on the device and no account is required to start practicing.
