This file is the operator template for Google Play reviewer access notes.

Do not paste this file into Play Console after account-linked premium ships.

Before submission:
1. Provision the dedicated reviewer premium account with `dart run tool/provision_reviewer_account.dart`.
2. Open `store/google-play/reviewer-access.generated.txt`.
3. Paste that generated file into `Policy and programs > App content > App access`.

Why this changed:
- Core app flows still do not require sign-in.
- Premium is now account-linked.
- Google reviewers need a dedicated pre-seeded premium account to actually use premium features, not just reach the paywall.

Required inputs for the provisioning step:
- `FIREBASE_WEB_API_KEY`
- `FIREBASE_PROJECT_ID`
- reviewer email
- reviewer password

The generated note tells reviewers:
- where to sign in: `Main Menu > Settings > Account`
- where to verify premium: `Main Menu > Settings > Chordest Premium`
- how to test account deletion separately with a throwaway account
